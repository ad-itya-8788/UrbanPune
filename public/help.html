Help & Support – Urban Pune

Need help using Urban Pune? We’re here for you! Below are answers to common questions.

1. How to Post a Room?

  • Go to the Post Ad section.
  • Fill in details like location, rent, description, and contact info.
  • Upload room images (optional) and click on Submit.

2. How to Contact the Owner?

  • Open any room ad you're interested in.
  • You’ll see the owner’s contact number or a WhatsApp/chat button.
  • Click to connect directly with them.

3. How to Delete, Edit or Hide My Post?

  • Log in to your account and go to your My Posts / Dashboard.
  • Click Edit to update your ad anytime.
  • Click Delete to permanently remove your post from the platform.
  • If your room is already full, click Make Private to hide the post from others.

4. I See a Fake or Suspicious Post

Please let us know immediately. We’ll verify the listing and take appropriate action to maintain trust on the platform.
