About Pune Rooms

Pune Rooms is a free and simple platform for people in Pune looking to share or find rooms, flats, PGs, and hostels directly from owners — no brokers, no commission.

Why We Started

Finding a room in Pune can be confusing and expensive. We created a platform where you can post genuine room ads or find roommates easily — just like scrolling Instagram.

What We Do

We don’t take any commission. We’re not brokers. We provide a digital notice board where you post your space, and someone finds it and contacts you — simple and direct.

Who Is It For?

  • Students searching for PGs or shared flats in Pune
  • Working professionals looking for rooms near offices
  • Owners or tenants who want to list available space

Our Vision

We aim to become Pune’s most trusted platform for room sharing and rentals — with real people, real listings, and zero middlemen.
